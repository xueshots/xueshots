Dan Xue

At home, his passion for high-octane sports drives him to capture professional athletes at annual Redbull events, marathons, and local boxing gyms. A boxer himself, Dan isn't afraid to break a sweat alongside his subjects.

Abroad, he brings over a decade of mountain backcountry expertise to every continent he lands in. Whether off-roading across alpine deserts at 13,000 feet or backpacking along rainforest coastlines, Dan prioritizes documenting the environment while honoring local people and cultures.

Dan practices ethical wildlife photography and uses his images to raise awareness of climate change and environmental stewardship.
